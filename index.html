As part of my design and development practice, I am subscribed to a platform called are.na similar to image collecting platforms like Pinterest but with a different business, philosophy and even algorithmic model.01 In that case, no algorithm to present a user "fitting" content and images/visuality/narratives.

"All creative works that can be put into digital form can be linked in a way that depends on nothing but the intellectual content of the material itself - no reliance on remote servers or any organization. It is simply a mathematical function on the actual intellectual content, and people would need nothing more than the function."[01]

As part of my research I save interesting images, layouts, references for the design process, and also links to websites as inspiration, collection, curation.
